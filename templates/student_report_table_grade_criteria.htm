Grade criteria 等级标准
1. Very poor 较差
2. Poor 不足
3. Average 一般
4. Satisfactory 满意
 		5. Good 良好
6. Very good 优良
7. Outstanding 优异

*Please note that Attainment grades relate to students of a similar age anywhere in the world. CAL/EAL relates to second language learners only.
*注意：该等级标准是相对于全世界同龄孩子水平而言。 CAL/EAL一栏的成绩只涉及非母语学习者。
